Christmas And New Year Office Arrangements

In preparation for the Christmas and New Year break please note the following:

Office closure

Our offices will be closed over the Christmas – New Year period from Thursday 24th December (Christmas Eve) through to Sunday 3rd January 2016.

We will be open again on Monday 4th January 2016.
Support

Emergency support will remain available for any business critical issues during this period. In the event this is required please call +61 3 9663 3554 and listen to the recorded message.
Change Management

In line with most of our customers’ procedures, as well as the wider industry, an embargo period will be in place during the period Friday 18th December though to Sunday 3rd January 2016. Any change requests during this period will be reviewed when the office reopens in the new year.
If you have any questions please call me on +61 3 9999 3128.

On behalf of us all at Inomial, I wish you a very Merry Christmas and Happy New Year.

Cheers,

Michael O’Leary
